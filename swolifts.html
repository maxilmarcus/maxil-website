Smarter Sets.
Heavier Reps.

Offline workout tracking for lifters who want fast logging, useful progress charts, and full control of their training data.

Explore SwoLifts Download
Featured Product

A powerful, offline first workout tracker for Android. Log sets, track progress, and crush your goals with no account required.

Progress Charts

Track strength trends with personal records, exercise charts, weekly volume, muscle volume, bodyweight, estimated 1RM, and training streaks.

Smart Logging

Log reps, weight, duration, and distance with automatic saving, set timestamps, and rest timers. A live notification keeps your workout timer running even when you switch apps.

Plate Calculator

Calculate exactly which plates to load for your target weight, bar weight, and available plates before the next set.

Workout Templates

Save favorite routines and start workouts with a single tap, including preloaded exercises and target set details.

Effort Tracking

Record reps in reserve when you want deeper context for hard sets and more useful estimated strength trends.

Exercise Library

Comes preloaded with nearly 400 exercises across all muscle groups. Add your own custom exercises anytime.

Accurate Load Types

Handle external weight, bodyweight, weighted bodyweight, and assisted bodyweight so volume reflects the movement.

Workout History

Browse workouts in list and calendar views. Edit past sessions, review stats, and track consistency over time.

Offline Data Ownership

No account or cloud dependency. Export and import your workouts, exercises, templates, and timestamps anytime.

About Maxil

Maxil is a software company focused on building thoughtful, well crafted tools for fitness enthusiasts.

We believe the best apps are the ones that get out of your way and let you focus on what matters: your training.

  • Privacy first Your workout data stays on your device. No accounts, no tracking.
  • Offline by default Works everywhere, no internet needed.
  • Built for lifters Designed by someone who actually uses it in the gym.

Your Data, Your Way

SwoLifts stores everything locally on your device. Export your data as a portable ZIP anytime. Use the Import & Export Guide when you want to edit templates on a computer or bulk migrate workout history.
