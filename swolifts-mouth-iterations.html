SwoLifts — Mouth Iterations

Goal: keep the gritted-teeth-as-barbell concept but make the clench fiercer and legible at app-icon sizes. Current mouth uses soft S-curves and 6px detail strokes that blur below ~64px. Variants below use straighter bar geometry, fewer/bolder teeth dividers, and (B/D) a downward snarl bow. Each is shown at 200 / 64 / 32px, plus on dark.
