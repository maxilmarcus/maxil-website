About Maxil

Maxil is a software company focused on building thoughtful, well crafted tools for people who want control over the data they create.

The goal is simple: make important information easier to capture, easier to understand, and easier to own.

  • Privacy first Products are designed to avoid unnecessary data collection and account barriers.
  • Portable by design Data should remain accessible, exportable, and useful outside the app.
  • Built by one, used by one Products are designed by an engineer who uses them directly, so decisions are grounded in real workflows, not assumptions.
  • Built for real use Features are shaped around repeated workflows, not demo screens.

Product Philosophy

Maxil products should turn everyday data entry into long-term access, better decisions, and more room to build on what users already know.
