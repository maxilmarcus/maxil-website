SwoLifts

A powerful, offline-first workout tracker for Android. Log sets, track progress, and crush your goals — no account required.

📊

Progress Charts

Visualize your strength gains with detailed charts for weight trends, weekly volume, estimated 1RM, and personal records.

📝

Smart Logging

Log reps, weight, duration, and distance with auto-save. Rest timers trigger automatically between sets so you stay focused.

📅

Workout History

Browse your complete workout history with a calendar view. Edit past sessions, review stats, and track consistency over time.

📋

Templates

Save your favorite routines as templates and start workouts with a single tap. Pre-populated exercises make logging fast.

Export & Backup

Your data is yours. Export everything as a ZIP file and import it back anytime. No cloud dependency, no lock-in.

315+ Exercises

About Maxil

Maxil is a software company focused on building thoughtful, well-crafted tools for fitness enthusiasts.

We believe the best apps are the ones that get out of your way and let you focus on what matters — in this case, your training.

  • ✓ Privacy-first — Your workout data stays on your device. No accounts, no tracking.
  • ✓ Offline by default — Works everywhere, no internet needed.
  • ✓ Built for lifters — Designed by someone who actually uses it in the gym.

Your Data, Your Way

SwoLifts stores everything locally on your device. Export your data as a portable ZIP anytime. We don't collect, store, or sell your information — ever.
