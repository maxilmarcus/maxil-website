SwoLifts

A powerful, offline first workout tracker for Android. Log sets, track progress, and crush your goals with no account required.

Progress Charts

Visualize your strength gains with detailed charts for weight trends, weekly volume, estimated 1RM, and personal records.

Smart Logging

Log reps, weight, duration, and distance with automatic saving. Rest timers trigger automatically between sets so you stay focused.

Workout History

Browse your complete workout history with a calendar view. Edit past sessions, review stats, and track consistency over time.

Templates

Save your favorite routines as templates and start workouts with a single tap. Preloaded exercises make logging fast.

Export & Backup

Your data is yours. Export everything as a ZIP file and import it back anytime. No cloud dependency, no lock in.

315+ Exercises

Comes preloaded with over 315 exercises across all muscle groups. Add your own custom exercises anytime.

About Maxil

Maxil is a software company focused on building thoughtful, well crafted tools for fitness enthusiasts.

We believe the best apps are the ones that get out of your way and let you focus on what matters: your training.

  • ✓ Privacy first Your workout data stays on your device. No accounts, no tracking.
  • ✓ Offline by default Works everywhere, no internet needed.
  • ✓ Built for lifters Designed by someone who actually uses it in the gym.
