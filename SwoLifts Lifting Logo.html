My ranking for SwoLifts + Lifting mark:
1. Bebas Neue — all-caps SWOLIFTS reads as one strong block, very “swole.” Strongest fit for the gym energy.
2. Anton — condensed mixed-case keeps the camel hump (the capital L) which is part of the name's identity.
3. Sora ExtraBold — if you want a modern fitness-app vibe rather than a gym poster. Geometric humanist, slightly softer than Inter, more refined than Archivo Black. Pairs well with the rounded face mark.
4. Archivo Black — most contemporary if you want a tech/app feel.
Skip Inter (too neutral) and Space Grotesk (too friendly). The longer wordmark also means we may want to stack the lockup (mark above word) for compact uses — happy to mock that next. Tell me which to lock in and I'll finalize size/weight/spacing.
